Covid - 19

Four thing we have to do to protect ourself from corona virus attack

Posted by Fatan Aminullah on April 1, 2021

Wash Your Hand

Don't forget to always wash your hand before doing your activities.

Wear Your Mask

If you want to go outside, you have to wear your mask. Wearing a medical mask is preferable. Change your mask everyday, never use your mask more than a day

Social Distancing

Avoid the crowd, watch your distance with other people. If you see a crowd just go away.

No Shaking Hand

Avoid physical contact with other people. We can replace shaking hand with only giving a gesture.
